About

Graduated with a degree in Communications engineering. During my undergraduate studies, I have been exposed to multiple areas ranging from software, hardware and networking related courses. Through the software courses, I have learned object oriented design in java, developing projects ranging from basic GUI games to larger scale applications requiring multithreading and the use of client server architectures. From a networking perspective, I learned about the layers of the OSI model and their operation in tandem. Furthermore, I developed working knowledge of various protocols in use and how to best design and implement networks based on customer requirements. I was also introduced to the basics of network security and cryptography. The highlight of my undergrad, my 4th year project allowed me to combine my various skills. Throughout the course of the project, I implemented and tested various devices and sensors. In this process, I further developed my skills in software/hardware integration. I was also introduced to machine-to-machine (M2M) communications techniques, their use to facilitate specific functionalities and pub/sub techniques alongside multiple APIs and web technologies. Having completed my Undergraduate degree, i am now working towards a full-stack web dev ceritifcation from Free Code Camp.
